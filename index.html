Mahatma Gandhi

About

Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi was an Indian lawyer, anti-colonial nationalist and political ethicist who employed
nonviolent resistance to lead the successful campaign for India's independence from British rule. He inspired movements
for civil rights and freedom across the world.
